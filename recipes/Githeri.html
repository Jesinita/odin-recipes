Githeri

Githeri is a tasty Kenyan dish.

It is originally Kikuyu, although it is prepared throughout Kenya.

Ingredients

  • 400 g boiled maize
  • 600 g boiled beans
  • 2 medium sized tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 cloves garlic - finely minced
  • Half red pepper bell
  • 1 chilli beef cube
  • 1 tbs vegetable oil
  • 2 large carrots
  • 1 TBS curry powder
  • 1 TBS salt

Instructions

  1. In a large saucepan on medium heat add oil and fry onion and garlic
  • Add tomatoes until cooked and add the beef cube, curry powder and salt
  • Next stir in carrots,maize and beans
  • Simmer for about 10 mins stirring occasionally
  • Finally add the green pepper and remove from heat
  • Serve and enjoy!
