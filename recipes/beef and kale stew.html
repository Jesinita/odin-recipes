Beef and Kale Stew

<

This delicious dish is the daily 'bread' in most Zimbabwean homes.

Beef and Kale Stew is best served with Sadza in Zimbabwe or Ugali in Kenya.

Ingredients

  • 1 table spoon of vegetable oil
  • 1kg stewing beef cubed
  • 1 medium chopped onion
  • 1 clove crushed garlic
  • 1 diced tomato
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 10 thinly chopped kale leaves
  • 1 beef cube
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Fry beef in oil until brown
  2. Add onion chopped onion and fry until brown
  3. Add pepper, salt and curry powder
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft and the beef stock and simmer for 20mins
  5. Add the kale and stir
  6. Add a bit of salt to taste
  7. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 mins
  8. Serve with sadza/pap/ugali
